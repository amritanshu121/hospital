Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and procedures

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment through our online booking system on the Appointment page, call us at +1-234-567-8900, or visit our hospital reception. Our online system is available 24/7 for your convenience.

What are your visiting hours?

Our general visiting hours are from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM daily. ICU visiting hours are from 11:00 AM to 1:00 PM and 4:00 PM to 6:00 PM. Please check with the nursing station for specific department hours.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans including Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, Cigna, UnitedHealthcare, and Medicare. Please contact our billing department to verify your specific coverage.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring a valid photo ID, your insurance card, a list of current medications, any relevant medical records, and your co-payment if required. Arrive 15 minutes early for check-in.

Do you have emergency services?

Yes, our Emergency Department is open 24/7, 365 days a year. For life-threatening emergencies, call 911. For urgent but non-life-threatening conditions, you can visit our ER or call +1-234-567-8900.

Can I get my test results online?

Yes, we offer a secure patient portal where you can access your test results, medical records, and communicate with your healthcare team. You'll receive login credentials after your first visit.

What parking options are available?

We offer free parking in our main parking garage with over 500 spaces. Valet parking is available at the main entrance for $5. Patient parking is validated for up to 4 hours.

Do you offer telemedicine services?

Yes, we offer virtual consultations for certain conditions and follow-up appointments. Contact your doctor's office to see if your appointment is eligible for telemedicine services.

How can I request medical records?

You can request medical records through our patient portal, by calling our Medical Records department at +1-234-567-8901, or by completing a records request form available at our reception desk.

What COVID-19 safety measures are in place?

We follow all CDC guidelines including enhanced cleaning protocols, social distancing measures, and health screenings. Masks may be required in certain areas. Please check our website for current policies.

Do you have a pharmacy on-site?

Yes, we have a full-service pharmacy located on the first floor near the main lobby. Our pharmacy is open Monday-Friday 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM and Saturday 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM.

How do I pay my bill?

You can pay your bill online through our patient portal, by phone at +1-234-567-8902, by mail, or in person at our billing office. We accept cash, checks, and all major credit cards.

Still have questions?

If you couldn't find the answer you're looking for, please don't hesitate to contact us.
